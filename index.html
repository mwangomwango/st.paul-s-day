ST. PAUL'S DAY SECONDARY

St. Paul’s is a Catholic Mission Day Secondary School, located in Mbala near the Moto-Moto
Museum in Zambia, has a rich and storied history that dates back to its establishment in 1953.
Originally founded as a skills center for carpentry, the institution has since evolved into a reputable
primary and secondary school that has made significant contributions to the education sector in the region.
The school's origins can be traced back to the mid-20th century when it was established as a vocational training center focusing on carpentry skills.
The establishment of the skills center was a response to the growing need for vocational training opportunities in the area, as the demand for skilled carpenters and craftsmen was on the rise.
The center provided valuable training to individuals seeking to acquire practical skills in carpentry, enabling them to secure employment and contribute to the local economy.
In 1970, the skills center underwent a transformation and was converted into a primary school, catering to the educational needs of young learners in Mbala and the surrounding areas.
This transition marked a significant milestone in the school’s history, as it expanded its scope to offer formal primary education to students, laying the foundation for its future growth and development.
Over the years, St. Paul’s Catholic Mission Day Primary School gained a reputation for academic excellence and a commitment to holistic education,
nurturing students not only academically but also spiritually, emotionally, and socially. The school became a beacon of hope and opportunity for many students,
providing them with a supportive and conducive learning environment where they could thrive and reach their full potential.
In 2017, another pivotal moment in the school’s history occurred when it was upgraded to a secondary school, offering a comprehensive
secondary education curriculum to students up to grade 12. This upgrade was a testament to the school’s continued growth and success,
reflecting its commitment to providing quality education and preparing students for higher education and future careers.
Today, St. Paul’s Catholic Mission Day Secondary School stands as a testament to the power of education
and the transformative impact it can have on individuals and communities.
The school continues to uphold its founding principles of academic excellence, moral values, and community service,
instilling in its students a sense of purpose, integrity, and a desire to make a positive difference in the world.
Overall, the history of St. Paul’s Catholic Mission Day Secondary School is a testament to its resilience,
adaptability, and commitment to providing quality education to the youth of Mbala and beyond.
From its humble beginnings as a skills center for carpentry to its evolution into a reputable primary and secondary school,
the institution has left an indelible mark on the educational landscape of the region.
As it continues to grow and evolve, St. Paul’s Catholic Mission Day Secondary School
remains a beacon of hope and opportunity for generations of students to come.
